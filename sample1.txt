Welcome to the Local RAG Chatbot!

This is a sample document that demonstrates how the chatbot works.

Key Features:
- Processes documents locally on your computer
- No API keys or internet connection required
- Supports PDF, TXT, and Word documents
- Uses semantic search to find relevant information

How to use:
1. Upload your documents using the sidebar
2. Click 'Process Documents' to build the knowledge base
3. Ask questions about your documents in the chat

The system uses local machine learning models to understand and search through your documents.
